Annotate Your Proteome / Add Organism to STRING
Submit the entire proteome of a species. STRING will compute the interaction network, predict the protein functions (including GO terms and KEGG pathways) and upload it to the website. You will be able to browse and perform the analysis of your proteome through the web interface, via programmatic access (API), or bulk download all predictions.
